### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment was friendly and open. FCJ members were always willing to provide support whenever difficulties arose, even outside normal working hours. The workspace was organized and comfortable, which helped maintain focus and work efficiency.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor and admin team provided dedicated support, explained unclear topics carefully, and consistently encouraged questions. In addition, the admin team helped by providing materials and creating favorable conditions for learning and completing assigned tasks more effectively.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The practical work was not completely aligned with the knowledge gained at university. However, this created opportunities to explore new areas and broaden understanding beyond the original major. As a result, adaptability to the real working environment improved over time.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, several new skills were developed, including the use of work management tools, teamwork, and communication in a professional environment. The mentor also shared practical experience that helped provide clearer direction for future development.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The working culture was positive, with mutual respect and a strong sense of responsibility among team members. At the same time, the environment remained comfortable and open, which helped build motivation and stronger team connection.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The organization offered flexible time support when needed, which created favorable conditions for interns during the internship period. In addition, being able to join workshops and knowledge-sharing events was a valuable benefit that contributed to broader learning and practical exposure.

### Additional Questions

- **Most satisfying aspect of the internship:** A friendly and open working environment together with enthusiastic support from the mentor and team.
- **What should be improved for future interns:** More detailed and closer guidance during the early stage would help interns adapt to the work more quickly.

### Suggestions & Expectations

- Strengthen onboarding support so interns can become familiar with workflows and practical tasks more quickly.
- Continue maintaining workshops, knowledge-sharing events, and learning support activities throughout the internship period.
