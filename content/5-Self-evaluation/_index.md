During the internship at **Bootcamp - First Cloud AI Journey @ AWS Study Group** from **05/01/2026** to **05/04/2026**, the real working environment provided an opportunity to learn and apply previously acquired knowledge to a specific project.

During the internship, **LunchSync** was developed as a platform that helps groups of office workers decide "what to eat for lunch" within three minutes. The project focuses on addressing issues such as **decision fatigue** and **conflict of preferences** through **Binary Choice Voting**, **Algorithmic Matching**, and an interactive quick-voting experience.

In terms of work attitude, strong attention was given to following regulations, maintaining seriousness in work, and taking responsibility for assigned tasks. In addition, communication with colleagues when difficulties arose was treated as an important part of improving teamwork effectiveness.
